At the Research Group for Applications and Innovation in Multimodal AI (GRAI-UNSTPB), our team of researchers from the National University of Science and Technology POLITEHNICA Bucharest advances Multimodal AI and Romanian NLP through open benchmarks, specialized datasets, and deep learning models.

Projects

Open research resources built around our work: datasets, benchmarks, models, and source code intended for reuse by the community.
